## 🤝 Contributing
Contributions are welcome to ensure this monitoring tool remains reliable as government websites evolve.

### Areas for Contribution:
* **Scraper Stability:** Improving the XPath or Regex logic in `monitor.php` if the BfDI website layout changes.
* **Bug Fixes:** Reporting and fixing issues with character encoding (Umlauts) or data extraction.
* **Documentation:** Enhancing the legal context or setup instructions for other users.

### How to contribute:
1. **Open an Issue:** Discuss significant changes or report a broken scraper first.
2. **Fork & Branch:** Create a dedicated branch for your fix or feature.
3. **Submit a PR:** Provide a clear description of your changes and why they are necessary for maintaining compliance monitoring.

### Knowledge Sharing & Legal Discussion
We explicitly encourage using the [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/d0ubIeU/pims-monitor/issues) for an exchange on the **practical and legal implementation** of the EinwV. 

If you want to contribute beyond code:
* **Share Implementation Paths:** How do you handle the public key infrastructure (PKI) once a new PIMS is detected?
* **Legal Interpretations:** Discuss official announcements from the BfDI and BNetzA regarding the PIMS trust list.
* **Standardization:** Help define common data structures for PIMS-to-Website communication.
